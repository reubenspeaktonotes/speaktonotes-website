SpeaktoNotes vs Granola

Voice-to-text app vs AI meeting notepad

Granola is an AI meeting notepad — you type sparse notes during meetings and AI fills in the rest. SpeaktoNotes turns your voice into polished text for any purpose. Here's how they differ.

Where SpeaktoNotes Wins

  • Mobile-first — full native apps on iOS and Android. Granola is desktop-only
  • Use it for everything — emails, texts, social posts, lists, meeting notes, quick thoughts. Granola is meetings-only
  • 6 Pockets — structured templates for 6 different content types
  • iOS keyboard extension — dictate into any app system-wide (Pro)
  • Voice-first — just speak. No typing required. Granola requires you to type notes during meetings
  • On-the-go — record in the car, on a walk, in the office. Granola only works at your desk during meetings

Where Granola Wins

  • Meeting-specific AI — combines your typed notes with audio context for richer meeting summaries
  • Privacy-first meeting approach — no bot joining your call, no "recording" notification
  • Team collaboration — share meeting notes and transcripts with teammates
  • Hybrid input — type + audio creates more accurate meeting notes than audio alone
  • 90–92% transcription accuracy — top-tier for meeting contexts
  • Free tier — 25 lifetime meetings to try

The Verdict

Choose SpeaktoNotes if you need a voice-to-text tool that works everywhere — on your phone, at your desk, on the go. For emails, notes, messages, social posts, meeting recaps, and quick thoughts. Voice-first, mobile-first, all platforms.

Choose Granola if your primary need is AI-enhanced meeting notes at your desktop, and you like typing sparse notes during calls while AI fills in the rest. Great for teams who share meeting notes.

Different tools for different workflows. SpeaktoNotes is your everyday voice capture tool. Granola is your meeting notepad.
