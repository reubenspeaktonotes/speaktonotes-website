SpeaktoNotes vs ChatGPT

Dedicated voice notes vs general AI

ChatGPT can do voice conversations. SpeaktoNotes turns your voice into polished, organised, ready-to-use text. They're fundamentally different tools - here's why that matters.

Where SpeaktoNotes Wins

  • Purpose-built for voice notes - one tap to record, transcribe, enhance, and save. No prompting needed
  • 6 Pockets - say something once and get a formatted email, meeting note, social post, or list. ChatGPT requires you to write a prompt every time
  • Note management - folders, search, history, export. ChatGPT buries everything in chat threads
  • iOS keyboard extension - dictate polished text directly into Mail, WhatsApp, Slack, Messages (Pro)
  • Calendar integration - speak and create events (Pro)
  • Faster workflow - optimised for speed. Record, enhanced text, copy. Done

Where ChatGPT Wins

  • General AI capabilities - ChatGPT can answer questions, write code, analyse images, browse the web
  • Free tier available - basic access at no cost
  • Conversation mode - back-and-forth voice conversations with AI
  • Broader use case - not limited to voice notes

The Verdict

Choose SpeaktoNotes if you need a fast, dedicated tool that turns speech into polished, organised notes, emails, and messages every day. No prompting, no chat threads - just speak and go.

Choose ChatGPT if you need a general AI assistant that can also do voice conversations, and you don't mind manually prompting and organising outputs yourself.

SpeaktoNotes does one thing and does it exceptionally well. ChatGPT does everything - but none of it as seamlessly for voice-to-text workflows.
