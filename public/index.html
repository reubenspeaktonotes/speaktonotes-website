Frequently Asked Questions

What is SpeaktoNotes and how does it work?
SpeaktoNotes is an AI powered voice to text app that turns your spoken words into clean, formatted notes instantly. Available on Mac, Windows, iPhone, and Android, simply press record, speak naturally, and our AI transcribes and formats your notes automatically. Whether it's meeting notes, journal entries, emails, or to do lists, SpeaktoNotes uses advanced AI to deliver accurate transcriptions with smart formatting, so you spend less time typing and more time doing.
How accurate is the AI voice transcription?
SpeaktoNotes uses state of the art AI transcription technology to deliver highly accurate voice to text conversion across all your devices. The AI handles natural speech patterns, accents, filler words, and punctuation automatically. Beyond basic transcription, our AI formatting engine cleans up your notes, removing "ums" and "ahs," adding proper punctuation, and organising your thoughts into clear, readable text. The result is polished notes that read like you typed them.
What's the difference between the Basic and Pro plans?
The Basic plan ($14.99 NZD/mo annually) includes 250 minutes of AI transcription per month, AI formatting, role based workflows, and 5 GB of cloud storage, perfect for day to day voice notes. The Pro plan ($28.99 NZD/mo annually) is built for power users and includes 1,000 minutes per month, everything in Basic, plus a keyboard shortcut for instant recording, custom writing styles, custom workflows, calendar integration, voice AI chat, and 10 GB of cloud storage. Both plans come with a 20% discount when billed annually.
Can I use SpeaktoNotes for meeting notes, emails, and other workflows?
Absolutely! SpeaktoNotes comes with powerful built in role based workflows designed for real world use cases. You can transcribe meeting notes, draft emails, write journal entries, create to do lists, and more, all by voice. Pro users can create fully custom workflows tailored to their specific needs, set their own writing tone and style, and even integrate with their calendar to automatically tag notes to upcoming meetings. Simply speak, and SpeaktoNotes formats everything into the right structure for you.
What devices does SpeaktoNotes work on?
SpeaktoNotes is available on Mac, Windows, iPhone, and Android, so you can capture voice notes wherever you are. Your notes sync across all your devices, meaning you can record on your phone during a meeting and access the formatted notes on your computer later. Whether you're at your desk or on the go, SpeaktoNotes works seamlessly across all major platforms.
