Questions

What is SpeaktoNotes and how does it work?
SpeaktoNotes is an AI-powered voice-to-text app available on iOS, Android, Mac, and Windows. Just speak, and it transcribes your words using the latest AI transcription technology (10–25x faster than standard), then AI enhances the text into polished emails, notes, lists, messages, social posts, or meeting notes using smart templates called "Workflows." Pro users can also create their own custom Workflows from templates. Your account syncs across all devices.
What features are included?
All plans include 6 AI Workflows (Email, Text, List, Note, Social, Meeting), AI-generated titles, action item extraction from meetings, folder organization, full-text search, and one-tap copy to clipboard. Pro users can create custom Workflows from templates. The iOS version also includes a system-wide keyboard extension for dictating into any app.
How much does SpeaktoNotes cost?
Monthly: $14.99 NZD/mo (250 min, 5 GB). Annual: $11.99 NZD/mo - save 20% (250 min, 5 GB). Pro: $24.99 NZD/mo (1,000 min, 10 GB, priority support). All plans include AI enhancement and all 6 Workflows. Cancel anytime, no hidden fees. Minutes reset each billing period.
What platforms does SpeaktoNotes support?
iPhone & iPad (iOS 15+), Android, Mac (native app), and Windows. All platforms are fully featured with the same AI transcription, Workflows, folder organization, and account sync. The iOS keyboard extension lets you dictate directly into any app system-wide.
Is my data secure?
Yes. Audio and text are encrypted in transit and at rest. Notes are private and only accessible through your authenticated account. Payments are handled securely via Stripe. Storage is included in your plan (5 GB standard, 10 GB Pro).
