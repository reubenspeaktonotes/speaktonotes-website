Questions

Can I switch plans anytime?
Yes. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing period. No hidden fees or cancellation charges.
What happens if I go over my minutes?
Your minutes reset each billing period. If you need more minutes, you can upgrade to the Pro plan for 1,000 minutes per month - 4x the Basic allowance.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. There are no lock-in contracts. You can cancel your subscription at any time and continue using SpeaktoNotes until the end of your current billing period. No cancellation fees.
